About Shannon Percival Smith

Welcome to my e-portfolio!

Shannon Percival Smith (she/her) is a data scientist with a passion for using machine learning to improve psychological health outcomes. She holds a Data Science and Machine Learning Certificate from MIT and a Bachelor of Science in Honours Biology from the University of British Columbia. Shannon’s work focuses on leveraging data to design predictive models aimed at reducing psychological injury among paramedics, a goal she pursues in her role as Program Coordinator at British Columbia Emergency Health Services. Co-founder of TIN & VINTNER and former Business Data Analyst & Operations Team Lead at the Vancouver Division of Family Practice, Shannon combines her expertise in data analytics with a deep commitment to improving mental health services. She is currently pursuing a master’s degree while continuing to work full-time, dedicated to making data-driven insights accessible in healthcare.
